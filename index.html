Resources On Surviving A Zombie Apocalypse

Welcome! Here you will find resources that will help avoid sealing your fate in a zombie apocalypse as well as unique locations to be and not to be during the apocalypse.

Gear

By gear, we mean clothes and accessories, you don't want to be that person who's running around the streets naked, especially in cold weather, you can get yourself killed even before the zombies even touch you!

  • Tactical Vest - This vest can help you avoid getting injured and provides as a supply carrier for weapons, food, walkie talkies, and medical supplies.

  • Combat Gloves - These gloves can help you punch zombies in the face when being attacked.

  • Tactical Boots - These shoes can help you kick zombies in the face as well as providing support in harsh weather conditions and work well on kicking doors.

  • Backpack - This item can assist you with carrying either weapons, food, and other useful resources fundamental to surviving the epidemic

  • Binoculars - This item can help you look around far areas that are too difficult to see with the naked eye

  • Wakie Talkie - This device can help you locate other survivors as well as communicate with other survivors around the area

  • Helmet - This head gear will protect you when you are confronting a zombie especially since zombies will most likely try to bite you around your head

  • Mask - Covers your face and prevents you from being bit or scratched by zombies as well as avoiding to breathe the faul stench of dead corpses

    • Food

    I mean, it's food, bring anything that can help you at least have something to eat. Also, bring some water too, water can help you live too.

  • Tactical Sammich - this food source is your key towards making through days even weeks without frequently starving 24/7.

  • Water - Not only it can help quench your thrist, but it can help you when you do not have any food.

  • Canned Food - There might not be any restaurants around the area you're in, especially during a zombie apocalypse, so it's best to carry these at all times to avoid hunger.

  • Fruits - In case, you're camping in the forest, always keep on the look out for food, you may be lucky to find a few berries and other unique fruits!

  • Bread - It's a quick snack when you're feeling like you want one.

    • Weapons

    Weapons such as a crossbow, baseball bat, axe, shotgun, pistol, submachine gun, grenades, knives, and mines will definitely be the main weapons to help defend yourself from a herd of zombies or facing one 1v1.

    First Aid Kit

    This thing is your best friend, it has banadages, first aid spray, herbs, vaccines, pills, medicine, ice packs, and a flashlight. All of these, except the flashlight, will help you when you get youself injured, or get bitten by a zombie. The flashlight will come in handy when your exploring dark places, especially when you're going out at night or wonder across an old abandoned place .
